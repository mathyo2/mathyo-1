<p style="color:white;"  > <h2 style="color:white;"   > Lionel Messi, often regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time, has left an indelible mark on the sport. Born in Rosario, Argentina, Messi’s incredible talent was evident from a young age. He joined Barcelona’s La Masia academy, where he honed his skills and rose to global stardom. With unmatched dribbling, vision, and scoring ability, Messi led Barcelona to numerous titles, including ten La Liga and four Champions League trophies. After a historic career at Barcelona, Messi moved to Paris Saint-Germain and later Inter Miami, continuing to inspire fans worldwide.  <h1  style="text-align:center;"  > <p  style="color:white;"> <i>ronaldo</i> </p> </h1>
<hr><hr>
<h2 style="color:white;" >Cristiano Ronaldo,is one of the greatest footballers of all time. Born on February 5, 1985, in Madeira, Portugal, he began his career at Sporting CP before joining Manchester United in 2003. Under Sir Alex Ferguson, he won three Premier League titles and the UEFA Champions League. In 2009, he moved to Real Madrid, becoming their all-time top scorer with 450 goals. Ronaldo has won five Ballon d'Or awards and multiple league titles in England, Spain, and Italy. Known for his incredible athleticism, goal-scoring ability, and leadership, he has also led Portugal to victory in Euro 2016 and the 2019 UEFA Nations League. Ronaldo continues to inspire millions worldwide.</h2>
